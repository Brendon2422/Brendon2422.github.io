Mission

The main objective of the company is to create a professional guarding
unit with intelligent staffing which must aid in the safety and security of the public and enterprises which are able and kitted to handle security risks which might present itself. Rendering this service to our client base in the Johannesburg and Pretoria region with the utmost precision will see growth of the company rapidly exceeding all expectations.

• To provide professional, intelligent and courteous guarding (non-armed as well as armed) along with VIP protection guarding when so required
• To provide regular patrols within the designated client sites
• To provide guarding services to local enterprises
• To bring a differentiated service than what the general public is used to
• To be the provider of choice in security provision
• To include technologies in the intruder detection and electronic systems sector
• To provide VIP protection services to high profile clientele

Stealth is a company providing security services with a focus on superior security services. Key focus areas which sets us apart:
• Clear focus on client service within a rapid attendance.
• Additional services over and above guarding and VIP protection, amongst others, technical installations, armed escorts, VIP protection services.

Stealth by nature provides professional guarding to clients resulting in recurring monthly fees. Security services include mainly but not limited to: Guarding and VIP protection, event security, Electronic systems installations and maintenance thereof. There is a technical division inside Stealth. This division operates as a separate department within Stealth. The main focus here is to provide intruder detection systems, electric fencing, CCTV, and other electronic security solutions on offer to Stealth customers. The CCTV monitoring will also grow into off-site monitoring services which will enable the future planned control room to view direct streams from clients’ CCTV systems on a CCTV viewing platform.
